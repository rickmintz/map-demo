LLamasoft Map Exercise

Instructions -

  • Use one of the two dropdowns to select an airport by its code or location. (both dropdowns will stay in sync)
  • The selected airport will be shown centered on the map with a marker giving its location.
  • Hovering over the marker will raise a tooltip that gives more detailed information about the airport.
  • You may hide (and later unshow) the marker using the appropriate button.
